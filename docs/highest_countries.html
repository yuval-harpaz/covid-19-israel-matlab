Most affected countries, 13.10.2020

Deaths, deaths per million and daily toll. Each plot shows the top 10 countries for the corresponding category.

Deaths per million shows you how much a country is contaminated. Daily deaths per million shows you how active Coronavirus is. Small countries with less than one million inhabitants (San Marino) have a gittery line, because every death is more than 1 per million.
